They came for an event by GUDRUN355, literature

They came for an event

They came for an event- those who did not care to see all the planets gathered there... And constellations so close that you did not have to guess. Trace the sky with a trembling hand. Swim in the milky way in blinding, often binding particles of light or blue-black currents on that night... both frightened and amazed. A gravity that far outweighed the body and spirits (if one still retained that spark) soared... And those who did not care laughed and talked with people who had not lived for years- familiar faces in star mist-star dust eyes close, very close... Seemed so natural to speak to these- take their hands and rise above your own backyard. No rhyme but time to anticipate- should not dare anticipate the beauty of a dream's miracle But wait until tomorrow! There was no tomorrow.
GUDRUN355
